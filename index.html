News and Announcements

The month of October is our ward's opportunity to care for our meetinghouse. Please don't miss this chance to volunteer to keep His house clean and orderly!


The Primary Annual Service Project will be held at the stake center on October 14th at 6:00pm. All Primary children and their families are invited to help create cards and assemble activity kits for children in the hospital.


The Ward Fall Activity is October 25th from 6:00-8:00pm! A chilli and soup potluck will be served accompanied by carnival games, costume contest and parade, and a Trunk-r-Treat. Please bring your family and friends. All are welcome!
